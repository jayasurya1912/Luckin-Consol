About Us

Luckin Consol Logistics Pvt Ltd is a specialised LCL import consolidation company offering reliable, efficient, and cost-effective cargo movement from major global origins to India. With strong industry expertise and an expanding international network, we ensure smooth consolidation, transparent communication, and timely deliveries for our partners.

We specifically focus on supporting Freight Forwarders and Customs House Agents (CHAs) by providing dependable LCL import solutions that help them serve their customers across multiple industries with confidence and consistency.

Linking our actions directly to the customer's needs is the foundation of our service thinking.

In addition, our focus on compliance and documentation accuracy ensures that all shipments adhere to international trade regulations and local customs requirements. By minimizing risks and avoiding delays, we help our partners achieve smooth clearance and faster cargo turnaround. Our dedicated support team works closely with clients to resolve challenges promptly and efficiently.
