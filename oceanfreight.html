Luckin Consol Logistics Pvt Ltd

At Luckin Consol Logistics Pvt Ltd, our Ocean Freight services provide reliable and cost-effective solutions for transporting cargo across global trade routes. Whether it’s full container loads (FCL) or less-than-container loads (LCL), we ensure smooth and efficient shipping tailored to your business needs.

With a strong network of global carriers and partners, we offer flexible scheduling, competitive pricing, and optimized transit times. Our team manages everything—from booking and documentation to customs clearance and final delivery—ensuring a seamless experience.

We prioritize cargo safety and transparency, providing real-time updates and complete visibility throughout the journey. No matter the size or complexity, our solutions deliver reliability, efficiency, and peace of mind.
